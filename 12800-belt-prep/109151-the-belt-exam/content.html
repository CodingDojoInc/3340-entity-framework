The Belt Exam

When you are ready for the exam, your instructor will provide a code that contains 8 numbers and/or letters. Use the "Take Exam" link from the menu above to unlock the Belt Exam with this code.

This exam aims to test the competencies you have learned throughout the course. You can achieve the following belts based on the level of mastery you display and the time it takes you to complete the exam:

  • If you complete the exam in under 5 hours and receive a score of 9.5 (out of 10) or higher, you will receive a Black Belt.
  • If you complete the exam in under 5 hours and receive a score between 8 and 9.49, you will receive a Red Belt.
  • If you complete the exam between 5 and 24 hours and receive any passing score (8 or higher), you will receive an Orange Belt.
  • If you take longer than 24 hours or receive a score below an 8, you will fail the attempt and can retake a new exam up to 2 additional times.

Important tips:

  1. Get some sleep the night before!
  2. Plan to dedicate the day to taking the exam (make a meal plan to eat while you take your exam if needed)
  3. Make sure you have 90% or more of the Core assignments completed before attempting the exam.
  4. Try at least one of the Belt Prep assignments before attempting the exam.
  5. Do whatever you need to destress or hype yourself up before starting. A stressed-out brain is capable of far less than a relaxed one!
  6. Believe in yourself! You have come a long way and you are almost there!
