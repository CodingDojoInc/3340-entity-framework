The Belt Exam

When you are ready for the exam, your instructor will provide a code that contains 8 numbers and/or letters. Use the "Take Exam" link from the menu above to unlock the Belt Exam with this code. You will have 24 hours to complete and submit the exam. If you go over your time limit the platform will not allow you to submit the exam. If you run short on time, consult your instructor for further instructions.

The belt exam will be scored according to the following rubric. You must earn at least 8 out of 10 points to pass. Your instructor will either grade it with you or provide written scores and feedback based on the rubric below:

  • To achieve an orange belt, you must score between an 8 and a 9.49 and submit within 24 hours. (This equates to a red belt on the rubric.)
  • To achieve a black belt, you must achieve a score between 9.5 and 10 within 5 hours and must successfully deploy your exam to an AWS EC2 instance within 24 hours. You must achieve both of these to qualify.

You have 3 chances to pass your exam before the final available exam day. Check your course schedule to see what the last day is to take the exam and consult your instructor for further details on retaking and cutoff times.

Important tips:

  1. Get some sleep the night before!
  2. Plan to dedicate the day to taking the exam (make a meal plan to eat while you take your exam if needed)
  3. Make sure you have 90% or more of the Core assignments completed before attempting the exam.
  4. Try at least one of the Belt Prep assignments before attempting the exam.
  5. Do whatever you need to destress or hype yourself up before starting. A stressed-out brain is capable of far less than a relaxed one!
  6. Believe in yourself! You have come a long way and you are almost there!

Good luck programmers!
